FAQs

What is FasTrip all about?

FasTrip is a leading online travel website that provides you a platform to search more than 700 different travel websites. It ensures your money saving, time saving and helps you to travel more.

Do I have to pay something to FasTrip?

No, FasTrip is absolutely free service and you don't have to pay anything.

Where I'll pay for my reservation?

As soon as you choose any website that suits your demands, you'll be tranferred to the corresponding website and there only you have to pay.

What about Billing system, Refund Policy and Modification in reservations?

All such inquiries can be initiated with the reservation provider. Please contact the concerned website for their policies.

What in case of any dispute to my reservation?

FasTrip is not responsible for any disputs in reservations. Please reserve your ticket with reliable website. FasTrip is associated with all the reliable websites.
